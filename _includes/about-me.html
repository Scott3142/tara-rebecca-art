About me



I’m Tara, I live in Mid Wales with my husband and our four beautiful boys.

I’ve always had a passion for drawing and creating something just that little bit different. I got into drawing digitally when Bridgend College asked me if I could draw some of their robots in their Robotics Department. I really enjoyed this and I even got to help them out on one of their showcase events in a local school.

Since then I’ve not really stopped drawing; from dogs, to buildings, to Disney characters and now my own style of cartoons. This has been a lovely journey for me and everyone I’ve encountered since starting up Tara Rebecca Art has been nothing but helpful.

This job has been incredible so far and I hope you take the opportunity to look through just some of my work, either on here, on Etsy, Facebook and Instagram.
